Home · Docs · Channels

Five channels, one request model.

Email, SMS, WhatsApp, push, and webhook all use the same platform contract and differ only in routing and provider configuration.

Email

Transactional and lifecycle mail

Templates render subject and body, and provider callbacks reconcile delivery status when supported.

SMS

OTP and alerts

Supports multilingual templates and provider pools such as Gupshup or Karix.

WhatsApp

Media, templates, and replies

Delivery callbacks and inbound replies are normalized into the same control-plane event model.

Push

FCM-backed notifications

FCM service accounts stay mounted as secrets while the request only carries the device token.
